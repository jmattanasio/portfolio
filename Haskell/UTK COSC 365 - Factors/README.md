Assignment
You are tasked with writing a Haskell program that will calculate the factors of a given range of numbers. The factors include one and the number itself. 

You will need to read two lines from the console. The first is the start number, the second is the end number. You will calculate the factors for all numbers [start..end]. You will need to print each number on its own line in a right-justified field of 5 characters, followed by a colon and space ‘: ‘, followed by the factors separated by a space. The last factor, which is the number itself, ends with a newline instead of a space. 

Testing 
Given starting as 1 and ending as 5: 
    1: 1 
    2: 1 2 
    3: 1 3 
    4: 1 2 4 
    5: 1 5
